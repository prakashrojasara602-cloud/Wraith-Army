# Neon Wraiths 💀🔥
**The Ultimate 1.21.11 PvP AI Engine**

**Neon Wraiths** adds highly advanced, lag-free AI PvP bots to the game that operate natively on Minecraft's core engine without relying on server-heavy background commands. These relentless bots feature pro-player combat mechanics like flawless W-Tapping, unpredictable strafing, instant shield-breaking, and ultra-fast Auto-Totem and Nethpot reflexes.

---

## ✨ Key Features

*  ⚡ **100% Native & Zero Lag:** Operates directly through Minecraft's native `MoveControl` and AI systems. Spawn an army of Wraiths without destroying your server TPS!
* ⚔️ **God-Level Combat (Master Tier):** Fights with a massive **14 CPS**, utilizes 95% accurate **W-Tapping** for insane knockback, and perfectly **Strafes** left and right to dodge attacks.
* 🛡️ **Smart Weapon Swapping & Shield Breaking:** Instantly switches to an Axe to disable shields (90% success rate), and seamlessly swaps to a Mace for Wind Burst attacks when airborne.
* 🧪 **Immortal Survival:** Features lightning-fast Auto-Totem mechanics and Auto-Nethpot (Splash Potion of Healing) when health drops below 12.
* 💀 **Brutal Armor Bypass:** Custom damage calculation completely nullifies target I-Frames (invincibility frames), shredding through heavy Netherite Tank players in seconds.
* 🤖 **Dynamic Modes & Formations:** Control your AI! Set them to `ATTACK` to hunt down enemies, `FOLLOW` to act as your personal bodyguard, or arrange them in custom geometrical `FORMATIONS`.
* 🎭 **Player Spoofing:** Bots appear in the server Tab-List exactly like real players, complete with realistic movements.

---

## 🎮 How It Works

Simply spawn a Wraith Bot and set its mode!
- **Idle:** The bot roams naturally.
- **Follow:** Acts as your loyal bodyguard.
- **Attack:** The bot activates its V8 Combat Engine and ruthlessly hunts down hostile targets or specific players using pro-PvP mechanics.

*(Works flawlessly in Minecraft Version 1.21.11)*
